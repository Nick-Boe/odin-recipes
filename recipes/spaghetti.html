Description

Savor the classic taste of spaghetti with this timeless recipe. With its al dente pasta, robust tomato sauce, and savory meatballs, this dish brings together the flavors of Italy in a comforting and satisfying way. Whether you're cooking for a family dinner or hosting a casual get-together, this spaghetti recipe is sure to be a crowd-pleaser that will transport everyone to the heart of Italian cuisine.


Ingredients

  • 12 ounces (340g) spaghetti
  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef
  • 1/2 cup (40g) breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) milk
  • 1/4 cup (25g) grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup (15g) chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cups (500ml) tomato sauce
  • 1 can (14 ounces/400g) diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, breadcrumbs, milk, grated Parmesan cheese, chopped parsley, egg, minced garlic, and finely chopped onion. Mix until well combined.
  3. Shape the meat mixture into small meatballs, about 1 inch in diameter.
  4. Heat a large skillet over medium heat and add a drizzle of olive oil. Cook the meatballs until browned on all sides and cooked through. Remove the meatballs from the skillet and set aside.
  5. In the same skillet, add the tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and black pepper. Simmer the sauce for 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  6. Return the cooked meatballs to the skillet and simmer them in the sauce for an additional 5 minutes to ensure they are heated through.
  7. Serve the spaghetti by placing a portion of cooked pasta onto each plate. Top with a ladleful of the meatball sauce.
  8. Garnish with fresh basil leaves, if desired, and serve this classic spaghetti dish while it's still warm. Enjoy!

This spaghetti recipe brings together the timeless combination of pasta, savory meatballs, and a rich tomato sauce. The aroma of garlic and herbs fills the air as the flavors meld together, creating a dish that is both comforting and satisfying. Whether you're preparing a weeknight dinner for your family or hosting a casual dinner party, this spaghetti recipe is sure to impress.
